LETS MAKE PIZZA

INGREDIENTS:

  1. 500 gr of flour
  2. 5 gr of brewer's yeast
  3. 350 gr of water
  4. 1-2 spoon of fine salt
  5. 1 spoon of sunflower oil
  6. tomato sauce, basil and mozzarella

PROCESS:

Put the flour on a table and create a sort of hole inside it, to contain the brewer's yeast dissolved in the water you are going to put in, mix the flour, the yeast and the water. when the dough takes on consistency, add fine salt and sunflower oil. Put everything in a container and let it rise for a day in the refrigerator.

The next day, in the morning, take the dough and let it rise again, but at room temperature until afternoon, where you will spread it on a steel container, with sunflower oil underneath to prevent the dough from sticking to the pan. Turn on the oven at 250-300 degrees fan mode, put the pan inside, add the tomato sauce oil and fine salt let it cook for twenty minutes and then add the sliced ​​mozzarella with basil and cook for another 5 minutes, after this it remains only to eat it!
